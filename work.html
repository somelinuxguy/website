Wildly successful startup to provide reminder services to veterinarians. On-site Linux servers, MySQL, EDI, PERL, printing. Bought out by Antech.

By Design Publishing

Magazine for realestate agents. Humongous data sets in mysql, linux servers, EDI, PERL, various printing technologies. Bought out by NCI.

Medata

Workers compensation billing company. Hundreds of employees, a load of SaaS serevrs, and a mix of cloud and on-site servers running an embarassing amount of outdated technology (Cobol) being migrated to modern tech (Java).

Skill City Games

MMO Puzzle Game platform with entirely hand drawn art and gorgeously addictive puzzle games. Game client and servers in Windows with C# and MySQL datastore.

Rook Media

Online marketing technology consultancy. Managing massive clusters of email servers all over the globe, advertiser campaigns, create and deploy custom tools in PHP or Python to assist clients with their shady advertising operations.

Shipping Easy

Software to assist in order fullfilment for online retailers. Handled billions (seriously, we ran out of txn ids in pgsql all the time) of records in Ruby, PgSQL, Redis, AWS, and Rackspace. DevOps managed with ansible and Capistrano with tooling in Python.

Ripple Labs

Crypto Currency company turning in to FinTech services for massive banks. Enormous network running on a mix of bare metal and cloud, onsite, offsite, etc. We use NodeJS, Wordpress, Linux, Ubuntu, C++, Ruby, Python, Ansible, and Jenkins as well as many many other tools.

Yip Yip

Social media platform design to facilitate blogs linked with social posts, in a one-to-many model. Think of a broker posting to all her agents at the same time. Vast amounts of engineering went in to coordinating all the moving parts here, with deep integration of Wordpress. Ruby on Rails, React Native, AWS and Ansible combined with strict deployment processes.
